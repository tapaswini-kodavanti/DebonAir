Promoting a pollution-free environment

Air Pollution

Despite sustainability actions, air pollution still creates dangers for us today. Learning about different pollutants is the best solution to preventing any harm before it happens.

Carbon Monoxide

Carbon monoxide is produced from incomplete fossil fuel combustion. CO poisoning symptoms include vomiting, headaches, nausea, and shortness of breath.

Nitrogen Monoxide

Nitrogen monoxide comes primarily from car and automobile combustion. Promote sustainability by using bikes or electric vehicles.

Nitrogen Dioxide

Nitrogen dioxide comes primarily from car and automobile combustion. Promote sustainability by using bikes or electric vehicles.

Sulfur Dioxide

Sulfur dioxide is produced from the burning of coal. It is more prominent along the East Coast.

Particulate Matter

Particulate matter is produced from factories, cars, trucks, and construction sites. It can cause chronic illnesses if precaution is not taken.
